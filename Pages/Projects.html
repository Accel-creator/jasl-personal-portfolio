Undeadify

A 2D top-down loot and craft base game. You spawn in with plenty of characters to choose from and get the best gears to survive through the nights of endless undead, fortify your base to endure the onslaught and beat the final boss of the game to complete it.

Genre and Tags : Single-Player, Crafting, Survival and Top Down

Roblox Projects

Monaria

A 3D game made with Roblox Studio. You spawn in and claim a plot and collect monsters scattered all over the map. The game's goal is to be as rich as you can get while there are side contents like slaying Bosses and competing in the leaderboard against other Roblox players.

Genre and Tags : Multi-Player, Tycoon and 3D
